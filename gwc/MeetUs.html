Statistics

Girls Who Code has reached 500 million people through online resources, campaigns, books, and advocacy work

300,000 girls have been served through GWC's in-person programming

50% of the girls come from historically underrepresented groups in tech


Mission Statement

Our mission is to get more girls into the tech industry by providing resources for the girls to help them achieve their goals.

We create a sisterhood based on trust, respect, creativity, and support.

We work together on projects to learn more about coding while having fun!
